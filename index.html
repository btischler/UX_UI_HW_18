work.

East Atlanta Kids Club

The East Atlanta Kids Club is a non-profit organization designed to provide mentoring and enrichment for under-resourced girls and boys ages 7 to 17. When we started talking to them about their goals one of the untapped areas was individual donors. As we began to take a deeper look at their web presence with this goal in mind we noticed a number of untapped resources that we could help bring to the forefront. We dramatically increased the use of engaging photography, emphasizing the impact the club was having on the youth and brought their stories to the forefront. We always created an always on donation button so at any point those interested in contributing could do so from any page on the site. We also streamlined the information and eliminated the superfluous content that was not adding anything. The end prodcut is a simple and powerful testament to the great work the East Atlanta Kids Club is doing.

Department of Energy

Government websites are notorious for an enormous amount of content and a convoluted navigation system. Through this navigation the entire organization was reworked. The homepage and deeper pages were redesigned as well to provide more visual interest and consistency throughout.

chore.ly

chore.ly is an app concept designed to make the most menial of household chores easy, affordable and predictable. With a sleek and simple design, chore.ly allows you to find trusted and vetted help, book and pay directly and set up recurring bookings. The beauty is in the simplicty that many other household apps lack.

travelEAZE

travelEAZE is a mobile travel app concept design. As anyone who travels frequently knows, there are way too many details to keep track of. travelEAZE lets you store all of your information in a single place and set notifications so that you can remember important details and not miss any meetings or flights.
